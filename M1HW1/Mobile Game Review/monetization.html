Monetization

The developer, Voodoo, makes money from this free to play title by overcrowding the UI (User Interface) with advertisments. After the completion of each level, a 15 to 30 second video advertisment will be displayed, preventing you from completely enjoying the gameplay. In order to completely rid of this screen junk is to pay:

$2.99

The game provides revivals in exchange to watch advertisments. If you don't want to pay the $2.99, you can recieve additional revivals:

$0.99

5 Minute Session

Minute 1--- 3 ads
Minute 2--- 1 ad
Minute 3--- 3 ads
Minute 4--- 4 ads
Minute 5--- 2 ads


SCORE:


2/10

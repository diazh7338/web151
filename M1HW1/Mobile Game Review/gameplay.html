Gameplay





WHAT DO YOU REALLY DO?

In Helix Jump, the player continuously completes vibrant and colorful levels that increasingly get more difficult as the player goes on. To complete levels, the player must navigate a ball - which in fact creates a satisfying "squish" noise - through floor openings while avioding to touch red areas.

You will know that you have completed a level when there are no more floors to jump through; however, the player seamlessly and endlessly enters additonal levels.

If the player manages to touch red areas, the game will prompt you to end your streak or watch an advertisment to further progress your current game session.

BUT...FUN?

Though Helix Jump can be very repetitive, that is also why it is so amusing...for a while. This game is great to simply pass some time, but more than likely, the user will fade out interest for its gameplay mechanics.

Helix Jump is in dire need to increase difficulty faster throughout each level. Currently, difficulty increase takes too much time to truly feel challenged.
